Super Yummy Chicken Salad

Description

This Chicken Salad is so good! Protein-packed with a delicious creamy dressing, and perfect for an on-the-go lunch in a sandwich or wrap.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup mayo
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3/4 onion powder
  • 1/2 garlic powder
  • 2 1/2 cups cooked chicken, diced
  • 1 1/2 cups red grapes, sliced
  • 1 cup finely diced celery
  • 1 cup roasted cashew halves and pieces

Steps

  1. Whisk the dressing ingredients until smooth. Taste and adjust, keeping in mind that it should taste salty / concentrated as it will distribute amongst all the other ingredients.
  2. Toss your mix-ins (chicken, grapes, celery, and cashews) with as much of the dressing as you like.
